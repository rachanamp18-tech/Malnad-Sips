About Us

Taste of Malnad is inspired by the serene hills and aromatic coffee plantations of Karnataka's Malnad region. Our passion lies in serving freshly brewed coffee alongside traditional Malnad cuisines prepared with authentic ingredients and time-honored recipes. We aim to offer a peaceful and comforting experience where nature, flavor and culture come together.
